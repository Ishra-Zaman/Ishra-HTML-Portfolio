About Ishra Zaman:

Ishra Zaman is a 13-year-old girl who was born in Queens, New York, but is currently living in Northern Virginia. She has an interest in STEM, specfically Science and is fascinated by the field of neuroscience and the human brain. In the future, Ishra hopes to become a neurosurgeon to help people suffering from neurological disorders.

Ishra is interested in the Engineering aspect of STEM as well. She gained an interest in coding very recently, particularly HTML. She is a beginner in HTML, and has been practicing coding with this language. She enjoys coding with HTML and hopes to increase her knowledge further in HTML and other coding languages.

One of Ishra's many hobbies are baking. Baking is a creative outlet for her and she enjoys experimenting with new recipes and flavor combinations. She has baked many desserts, from classic chocolate chip cookies to elaborate layer cakes. She is always eager to bake whenever the oppurtunity arrives and loves trying new things in the kitchen. Ishra has even created her own book about her delicious baking recipes.

Another hobby of Ishra's is traveling. She traveled many times to other states and countries. Her most memorable places she traveled to were New York, Florida, Dubai, Saudia Arabia, and Bangladesh.
